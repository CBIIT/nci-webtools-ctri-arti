Welcome to Research Optimizer
Research Optimizer supports biomedical research by providing tools to manage, query, and transform biomedical data. In line with the mission of the Clinical & Translational Research Informatics Branch (CTRIB), our tools are designed to streamline workflows and make data more accessible across a range of use cases.
